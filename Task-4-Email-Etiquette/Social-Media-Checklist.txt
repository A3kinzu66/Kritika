Do’s
-Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments
-Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading misinformation)
-Maintain privacy by adjusting account settings appropriately
-Think before posting—consider long-term impact
-Use social media for learning, networking, and skill-building
Don’ts
-Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
-Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or controversial content
-Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
-Avoid oversharing daily activities or location details
-Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
