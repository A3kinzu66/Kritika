Stay Safe Online Checklist:
-Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone
-Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps
-Always verify the sender before making any payment
-Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
-Use strong and unique passwords for accounts
-Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
-Regularly update apps and devices for security patches
-Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
-UPI Tip: Remember—entering PIN = sending money
-UPI Tip: Double-check the receiver's name before confirming payment
Report Cybercrime:
-National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-Helpline: 1930
